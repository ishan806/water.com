WATER

Water is 1/3 part of earth's surface and (else is land), thus Earth is called as Blue planet. water is present in the form of, frost and snow, underground water, clouds, rivers, ponds, lakes and seas etc. the water which can be use by humans consumption. Rain water is the purest type of natural water on earth and sea water is unpurest type of natural water found on earth.
frost and snow --- it is water in solid form, it forms due to low tempreture, the snow on mountains when melt because increase in tempreture, it turn in liquid state making small streams and flow down through the slope, and these streams joins togather and forms larger streams, gragually they became bigger and bigger forming rivers, the speed of river increases as it flows down the slope.
river --- it is a type of unpure water which keeps flowing, a river can carry some kind of salt and mineral, a river have two parts source and mouth, a source of a river is the point where a river originate and a mouth is the point where a river flows into a sea.
sea --- it is the most unpure form of the water found on Earth, major part of water found on Earth is in sea, it's water is highly saline, A sea is a major medium of transportation, a huge percent of plants on Earth are found under seas. A sea is also a major source of evapouration, many minerals, substances and huge amount of salt is found in sea water which is distilled to obtain salt.
underground water --- it is found under the ground, full of minerals, which can be consumed by humans, it is take out by hand pumps and wells.
clouds --- it is is the form of water before rain, when tiny water droplets float in air togather, they forms a cloud, and when these droplets joins togather, they became bigger and there weight increase, thus they are not able to float in air and falls as rain.
